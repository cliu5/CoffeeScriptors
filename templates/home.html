How to Play

Poketica is a pokemon version of the application Habitica. It's perfect for people who have trouble staying on task.
Start by adding tasks to your taskbar. Rate their difficulty and indicate what kind of task it is. After completing a task, you can check it off from your taskbar. Checking off tasks will allow you to gain gold.
You can use gold you've gained to buy more pokemon TCG cards to catch them all . Go to inventory to see what pokemon you've bought.
